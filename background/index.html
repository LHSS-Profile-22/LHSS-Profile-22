Demographics

Gender

Women make up 17% of the students, which is approximately the same proportion as the number of women in tech. Although the gender ratio isn’t something to be satisfied with, the trend is positive: the proportion of female students in more-recent SE classes has been increasing.

Ethnicity

SE students come from many different ethnic backgrounds, from at least 27 different countries. About half of the student have ethnic origins in East Asia, while the remaining half consist mostly of Caucasians and South Asians.

High school extracurriculars

Less than 35% of students were involved in a STEM, CS or Robotics club during high school. Instead, students participated in all sorts of extracurricular activities such as volunteer work for non-profits, being a cadet, starting a school newspaper, and more. Pursuing their own unique personal interests was a common pattern amongst students during high school.

Level of education

There are more parents with Masters and PhD’s combined than Bachelors. Compared to their parents, only 3 students out of 113 chose to do grad school. This could either be a generational shift, or it could just be that students are tired of school after 5 years with no summer.
