Family Funding

80% of students received some form of funding from their family. For Canadians, a popular source of funding is a Registered Education Savings Plan (RESP). Other methods of funding could include assisting in tuition payments, rent payments or contributions to other living expenses.

This may suggest that the families of the majority SE students had already planned for their children to attend post-secondary education.
